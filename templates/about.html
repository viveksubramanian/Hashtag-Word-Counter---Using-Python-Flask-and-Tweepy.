What does this do?

This returns the top 100 words associated with a #hashtag for a specified period of time.

The streaming API provided by Twitter is used, to find the tweets that are generated by users around the world.

It is important to note that less useful words like for, to, etc. haven't been filtered

How do you use the API?

Enter a twitter #hashtag and duration, to find the top 100 words and the word count associated with the word.

What Frameworks/Packages have been used?

1. Python Flask Microframework

2. Tweepy - Collect Tweets
